**Null Hypothesis**: In the ==population==, there is no difference in outcomes between the groups.
	Odds ratio = 1
	Relative Risk = 1
	Risk difference = 0

**Alternative Hypothesis**: In the ==population==, there is a difference in outcomes between the groups.
	Odds ratio is not 1
	Relative Risk is not 1
	Risk difference is not 0
